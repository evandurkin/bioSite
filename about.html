Ying Chen (b. 1991) was born in FuJian, China. At the age of six she moved to New York City’s Lower East Side with her mother and older sister. Chen and her family spent their early years in the states moving between New York, Florida and Georgia. During this period, Chen found it difficult to adjust to a new language and environment. Balancing life between Manhattan's city life and the suburbs of Leesburg, Georgia was disorienting for Chen until she was able to adapt to a more mobile lifestyle.

In 2012 Chen and her family moved to Brooklyn. Chen was working towards pursuing her degree as a dental hygienist while her older and younger sisters were also completing their own studies. Chen’s mother also opened up a plant shop below their apartment when they moved back to Brooklyn. This was a family effort that everyone contributed towards to help set up and maintain the shop to its present state.

When Chen is not polishing enamel or pruning plants she’s usually working on her next photography project, which has been a lifelong hobby. Chen’s interest in photography first started when her old sister started taking amateur pictures around the house during their childhood. Over the years, Chen’s creative pursuits with photography have evolved and she now regularly photographs friends and family. Chen also enjoys other creative media such as paint-socials with her sisters a local paint store and she also enjoys creating videos for her YouTube channel in which she provides commentary to popular Chinese drama TV shows. She’s hopes to turn her creative pursuits into a career someday, either in the US or China.

Chen currently lives in Brooklyn with her mother, two sisters and two dogs.
